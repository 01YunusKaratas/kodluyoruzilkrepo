About Google

Google is a global technology company that specializes in internet-related services and products. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford University, Google has grown to become one of the world's most valuable and influential companies.

Our mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. We continually strive to create innovative technologies and provide services that improve the lives of people around the globe.
